Case Study

Rebuilding Revenue Infrastructure
DTC Skincare Brand · Shopify + Klaviyo

The Situation
  • Growth heavily dependent on Meta and influencer spend
  • AOV ~$18 limiting LTV potential
  • Deliverability issues causing poor inbox placement
  • No structured loyalty or retention engine
  • Weak subscription adoption, churn trending up
My Diagnosis

After auditing the funnel, the issue wasn't traffic. It was systems alignment, subscription UX, and lifecycle sequencing — none of which were designed to drive repeat behavior. Retention wasn't a growth lever. Paid had to carry everything.

Execution
  • Fixed deliverability via SPF, DKIM, DMARC + copy/cadence/segmentation cleanup
  • Migrated from Mailchimp → Klaviyo for cleaner lifecycle architecture
  • Rebuilt Shopify: nav, PDPs, premium layout, and subscription UX end-to-end
  • Implemented core flows: welcome, post-purchase, replenishment, winback
  • Coordinated packaging, pricing reset, and claims for premium positioning
  • Launched loyalty + insert strategy to drive reviews and repeat purchases
Outcome
  • Email/SMS became highest converting channel, outpacing paid ads
  • Unsubscribe rate dropped to near-zero after lifecycle restructure
  • Subscription adoption improved ~5 points through UX + framing changes
  • New haircare vertical launched on separate Shopify instance with full lifecycle stack
